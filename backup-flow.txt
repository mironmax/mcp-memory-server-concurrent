Intelligent Backup Rotation Flow
=================================

Time    Daily (7)         Weekly (4)        Monthly (12)      Yearly (∞)
----    ---------         ----------        ------------      ----------
Day 1   [backup-1]
Day 2   [backup-2,1]
Day 3   [backup-3,2,1]
Day 4   [backup-4,3,2,1]
Day 5   [backup-5,4,3,2,1]
Day 6   [backup-6,5,4,3,2,1]
Day 7   [backup-7,6,5,4,3,2,1]
Day 8   [backup-8,7,6,5,4,3,2] → [backup-1]
                                    ↑
                              (promoted from daily)

Day 35  [last 7 days]       [backups from      
                             week 1,2,3,4]
                                  ↓
                             (oldest weekly 
                              age 28 days)
                                  ↓
                             [monthly-1]

Month 12 [last 7 days]      [last 4 weeks]    [months 1-12]
                                                     ↓
                                                (oldest monthly
                                                 age 365 days)
                                                     ↓
                                                [yearly-1]

Year 2+  [last 7 days]      [last 4 weeks]    [months 1-12]    [year1,2,3,...]
                                                                 (kept forever)

Retention Rules:
- Daily:   Keep 7, delete older OR promote to weekly
- Weekly:  Keep 4, delete older OR promote to monthly  
- Monthly: Keep 12, delete older OR promote to yearly
- Yearly:  Keep forever (no deletion)

Promotion Conditions:
- Daily → Weekly:   Age ≥ 7 days
- Weekly → Monthly: Age ≥ 28 days (4 weeks)
- Monthly → Yearly: Age ≥ 365 days (12 months)
